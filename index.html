VINYL FENCING

Durable, low-maintenance fence that withstands Florida’s harsh weather and enhances your property’s style.

Vinyl Fence

A vinyl fence is a popular and attractive option for homeowners in Florida who are looking for a durable and low-maintenance fencing solution. Made from PVC (polyvinyl chloride), vinyl fences are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions, such as heavy rain, strong winds, and intense sunlight, without fading, warping, or cracking. One of the key advantages of vinyl fences is that they are highly customizable, with a wide range of colors, styles, and textures available to choose from. Whether you prefer a traditional picket fence or a modern privacy fence, you can find a vinyl fence that suits your aesthetic preferences and functional needs. In addition to their versatility and durability, vinyl fences are also easy to clean and maintain. Unlike wood fences that require regular staining or painting, vinyl fences can be cleaned with just soap and water, making them a cost-effective and hassle-free choice for homeowners. If you're looking for a stylish and practical fencing solution for your Florida property, a vinyl fence could be the perfect choice. CHAIN LINK FENCING

Affordable and reliable, it is perfect for securing residential and commercial properties.

Chain Link Fence

A chain link fence is a popular choice for both residential and commercial properties in Florida. It is a type of fence made of galvanized steel wires woven together to form a diamond-shaped pattern. This type of fence is durable, long-lasting, and provides a clear view of the surrounding area.

Chain link fences come in various heights and gauges to accommodate different needs and applications. They can be installed with different types of posts and hardware to ensure that they are sturdy and secure. Some chain link fences come with a vinyl coating that provides additional protection against rust, corrosion, and UV rays.

Chain link fences are easy to install and maintain, making them a cost-effective choice for many property owners. They are also versatile and can be used for a variety of purposes, including security, privacy, animal containment, and decorative purposes. They are commonly used around swimming pools, sports fields, playgrounds, and construction sites.

Overall, a chain link fence is a practical and affordable option for property owners in Florida who are looking for a reliable and low-maintenance fencing solution.

ALUMINUM FENCING

Elegant and durable, it offers beauty and security while requiring minimal maintenance.

Aluminum Fence

Aluminum fencing is an excellent choice for both residential and commercial properties in Florida. Made from high-quality aluminum alloy, these fences are lightweight, durable, and require very little maintenance. Here are some key features of aluminum fencing that make it a popular choice for Florida homeowners: Durability: Aluminum fencing is known for its strength and durability, and can withstand harsh weather conditions, such as hurricanes and extreme heat, which are common in Florida. Low maintenance: Unlike wood fencing, which requires regular staining and sealing, aluminum fencing requires minimal maintenance. It is resistant to rust, corrosion, and fading, and can be easily cleaned with soap and water. Customizable: Aluminum fencing comes in a variety of styles and colors, making it easy to match the aesthetics of any property. It can be customized to fit any height or width, making it suitable for any size of property. Security: Aluminum fencing can be installed with locking mechanisms and other security features, making it an ideal choice for commercial properties and high-security areas. Eco-friendly: Aluminum fencing is 100% recyclable, making it an eco-friendly option for those who are concerned about the environment. At YAJU FENCE, we offer a wide range of aluminum fencing options to meet the specific needs of our customers in Florida. Our team of experts can help you choose the perfect style, color, and height to match your property and ensure that your fence is installed correctly and securely. WOOD FENCING

Classic and timeless, it is built with high-quality cedar for long-lasting privacy and beauty.

Wood Fence

A wood fence is a classic and timeless choice for homeowners looking to add privacy and beauty to their property. Wood fences are available in a variety of styles, such as picket, privacy, and split rail, and can be customized to fit the specific needs of each homeowner. They are constructed of cedar, which is known for their durability and resistance to decay and insects.

Our specification includes:

  • 1×6 pickets
  • 2×4 rails
  • 4×6 gate hinge posts
  • 4×4 posts

We build your wood fence from scratch on site. We set every post in concrete so your posts won’t shift or lean. We nail up every board one by one using hot-dipped galvanized ring shank nails that won’t rust or pull apart.  This allows for a custom construction that is stronger than pre-built panels.

In Florida, wood fences are a popular option due to their ability to withstand the heat and humidity of the state's climate. The natural warmth and beauty of wood also complement the lush foliage and tropical landscapes of Florida. Whether you're looking to add privacy to your backyard or create a boundary around your property, a wood fence is a versatile and attractive choice for any Florida homeowner.

  • Horizontal Privacy Fence
  • Ranch Rail Fence
